About Us

Noah's Ark, the beloved dog and cat animal shelter in Angeles Pampanga, began its heartwarming journey as a humble dream nurtured by a few passionate animal lovers in the community. The shelter's origins can be traced back to a single act of compassion and a shared vision for a better life for the four-legged residents of the city.

With unwavering determination, these compassionate souls pooled their resources and began the challenging task of creating a sanctuary for these animals. They started with a small, makeshift shelter and a handful of volunteers who shared their passion for making a difference. Noah's Ark faced numerous obstacles on its path to becoming a thriving animal shelter. Financial constraints, limited resources, and the sheer magnitude of the problem they sought to address made the journey an uphill battle. However, their unyielding dedication and belief in their mission pushed them forward.

Through the years, the shelter slowly but steadily expanded, both in terms of physical space and community support. The local community began to recognize the importance of the shelter's work and rallied behind it, providing not only financial assistance but also volunteering their time and expertise.

Today, Noah's Ark stands as a testament to the unwavering spirit of its founders and the generous hearts of the community. It is a place where second chances are granted, where wounds are healed, and where countless dogs and cats have found loving forever homes.

OUR STANDARDS

Streamlined Adoption Process

Our streamlined adoption process is designed to connect you with your future pet efficiently, ensuring a stress-free experience for both you and your new family member.

Commitment to Animal Welfare

Our commitment to animal welfare is at the heart of everything we do. Every pet in our care receives the love, attention, and medical care they deserve.

Promoting Responsible Pet Ownership

Pet ownership is a lifelong commitment, and at Noah's Ark, we take this commitment seriously. We are passionate about promoting responsible pet ownership through education and support.

Building a Pet-Friendly Community

Noah's Ark extends beyond adoption; it's about fostering a vibrant and pet-friendly community. We believe in the power of connection and actively work to build a network of pet lovers.
